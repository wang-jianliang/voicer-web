Browse with Your Ears: Voicer - Where Text Comes Alive!

Voicer is a browser extension that brings your selected web text to life through speech. With Voicer, simply highlight any text on a webpage, right-click, and select the “Read this” option from the context menu. Within moments, Voicer converts the chosen text into natural-sounding speech and presents a sleek playback interface at the bottom of your screen.

By clicking the play button in the interface, you can listen to the text being read aloud, providing a seamless and efficient way to consume web content audibly.

Enhance your browsing experience and make reading online easier with Voicer.
